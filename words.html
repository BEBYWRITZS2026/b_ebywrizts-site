WORDS

On speech, consequence, and responsibility

WORDS Words are like knives, They shape and destroy lives, So be careful what you say, Because along the way you will pay, Or like a lamp that goes grey, Do have an iota of doubt, Before you open your mouth. Words are nice, But at the same time bite, Before you rant, Think of what you will attract, Then share your story And give God the glory.
